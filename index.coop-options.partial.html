HOUSES

Each House has its own noble history and each has produced outstanding witches and wizards.

Gryffindor

Gryffindor valued bravery, daring, nerve, and chivalry. Its emblematic animal is the lion, its representative gemstone was rubies, and its colours are scarlet and gold.

Application

Hufflepuff

Hufflepuff valued hard work, dedication, patience, loyalty, and fair play. Its emblematic animal was the badger, its representative gemstone was yellow diamonds, and yellow and black were its colours.

Application

Ravenclaw

Ravenclaw valued intelligence, knowledge, curiosity, creativity and wit. Its emblematic animal was the eagle, its representative gemstone was sapphires, and its colours were blue and bronze.

Application

Slytherin

Slytherin valued ambition, leadership, self-preservation, cunning and resourcefulness. Its emblematic animal was the serpent,its representative gemstone was emeralds, and its colours were emerald green and silver.
